Privacy Policy for Alibi

Effective Date: August 5, 2025

Alibi (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share personal information from users of our mobile application (“App”).

Information We Collect

  • User ID and Account Information (via Firebase Authentication)
  • Voice and Sound Recordings (for voice call processing and summaries)
  • Call Metadata (e.g., timestamps, phone numbers, transcripts)
  • Purchase Information (handled securely through RevenueCat and app store billing)
  • Approximate Location (via IP address, for personalization and fraud prevention)
  • Device Information and Identifiers (for analytics, performance, and fraud detection)

How We Use Your Information

  • To enable voice call answering and AI-generated summaries
  • To provide in-app purchase functionality
  • To monitor and improve app performance
  • To prevent fraud and ensure secure operations

Third Parties

We use trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) for authentication, crash reporting, and analytics
  • Twilio for voice call handling
  • OpenAI for generating call summaries
  • RevenueCat for managing subscriptions and purchases
